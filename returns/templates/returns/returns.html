Return & Refund Policy

Effective Date: May 30, 2025

Thanks for choosing Eco | Shop. If you are not completely happy with your purchase, we are here to assist you.

1. Returns

  • You have 31 calendar days to return an item from the day you received it.
  • To qualify for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition as when you got it.
  • Your item should be in its original packaging and include the receipt or proof of purchase.

2. Return Process

To start a return, please follow these steps:

  • Reach out to our support team at support@ecoshop.com with your order number and the reason for your return.
  • We will email you a return authorization along with instructions on where to send your package.

3. Refunds

  • Once we receive your item, we will inspect it and notify you of the status of your refund.
  • If your return is approved, we will process a refund to your original payment method.
  • Refunds may take 5 - 10 business days to show up in your account.

4. Shipping Costs

  • You wll be responsible for your own shipping costs when returning an item, unless the return is due to our mistake.
  • Shipping costs are non-refundable.

5. Exchanges

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. If you need to exchange an item, please contact us at support@ecoshop.com.
