Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 30, 2025

This Privacy Policy outlines how Eco | Shop gathers, utilizes, and safeguards the personal information you share while using our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Identification Information: This includes your name, email, phone number, and both shipping and billing addresses.
  • Payment Information: We securely process your credit or debit card details through trusted third-party gateways.
  • Account Data: This consists of your login credentials and order history.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To process your orders and deliver products to you.
  • To keep you informed about your orders, account status, or any customer support inquiries.
  • To enhance our website, products, and services.
  • For marketing purposes, but only with your consent
  • To meet our legal and regulatory obligations.

3. Sharing Your Data

We may share your information with:

  • Trusted service providers, like delivery services and payment processors.
  • Legal authorities when required by law.
  • Rest assured, we do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Security

We take your data security seriously and use top-notch industry standards to keep it safe. That said, it's important to remember that no method of online transmission is ever completely foolproof.

5. GDPR - Your Rights

If you're in the EU or UK, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) gives you several important rights, including:

  • The right to access your personal data
  • The right to correct any inaccurate or incomplete information
  • The right to request the deletion of your data
  • The right to object to or limit how your data is processed
  • The right to request your data in a portable format

If you want to exercise any of these rights, just reach out to us at support@yourshop.com.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

7. Children's Privacy

We don't intentionally collect data from anyone under 16. If we discover that we have, we'll delete it right away.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page along with the new effective date.
